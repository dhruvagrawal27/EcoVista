# 🎬 EcoVista — 2-Minute Demo Video Script

> **Format:** Screen recording + voiceover  
> **Total time:** ~2 minutes 10 seconds  
> **Tone:** Confident, clear, technical-but-accessible  
> **Tip:** Record at 1080p, use a microphone. Zoom in on key UI elements when narrating them.

---

## [0:00 – 0:10] HOOK — Landing Page

> **On screen:** Open the browser to the EcoVista landing page. Scroll slowly through the hero section.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Indian university campuses consume billions of kilowatt-hours every year, yet most have no real-time visibility into where that energy goes — or how much carbon they're emitting. EcoVista changes that."

---

## [0:10 – 0:22] LOGIN & ROLE SYSTEM

> **On screen:** Click "Log in." Show the login screen briefly. Log in as **Admin**. Show the role badge that appears on the Dashboard.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "EcoVista supports five user roles — Admin, Facility Manager, Finance, Faculty, and Student Lead — each with precisely scoped access. Watch as the dashboard adapts the moment I log in."

---

## [0:22 – 0:45] DASHBOARD — LIVE CAMPUS OVERVIEW

> **On screen:** Dashboard loads. Highlight the 4 KPI metric cards — Total Demand, Solar Generation, Carbon Today, Energy Cost. Hover over the trend arrows. Scroll down to show the energy chart, building rankings, and quick-action buttons.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "The dashboard is the command centre. Live metrics — current campus demand, solar generation, carbon emissions, and today's energy cost — all pulled from our Supabase database in real time. Trend arrows show how today compares to yesterday. Below, you can see which buildings are performing best, and the AI insight panel surfaces the most urgent action right now."

---

## [0:45 – 1:12] ENERGY INTELLIGENCE — FORECAST + WHAT-IF

> **On screen:** Navigate to **Energy**. Click the **Forecast** tab. Let the 72-hour chart load with the confidence bands visible. Then click the **What-If Simulator** section — drag the Temperature slider up, then the Occupancy slider. Show the estimated demand shift updating.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "The Energy module is where the ML models shine. This 72-hour forecast is generated by an LSTM neural network trained on two years of campus data — those shaded bands represent the 90% confidence interval. And with the What-If Simulator, facility managers can ask: if tomorrow is 5 degrees hotter and occupancy doubles — how much extra demand should I expect? The model answers in real time."

> **On screen:** Click the **Buildings** tab. Click on one building — show the radar scorecard and the retrofit suggestions list with payback years and carbon reduction.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Drill into any building for a full performance scorecard — HVAC health, carbon score, EUI — and see AI-suggested retrofits with predicted payback periods and carbon savings, scored by our Gradient Boosting models."

---

## [1:12 – 1:28] MISSION CONTROL — NET-ZERO SIMULATOR

> **On screen:** Navigate to **Mission Control**. Toggle on "100% Solar Expansion" and "AI Demand Response." Drag the intensity slider to 80%. Watch the trajectory chart animate — show the net-zero year jumping forward.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Mission Control lets decision-makers simulate the path to net zero. Toggle decarbonisation levers — solar expansion, EV fleet, demand response — set implementation intensity, and the trajectory chart recalculates live. Right now, with these two levers at 80% intensity, we accelerate net-zero by almost a decade."

---

## [1:28 – 1:42] AI INSIGHTS + AI ASSISTANT

> **On screen:** Navigate to **Insights**. Show the ranked recommendation cards — ROI %, carbon impact, ease score. Click "Sort by ROI." Then open the **AI Command Panel** (bottom-right bot icon). Type: *"What's the highest ROI action we can take this month?"* — show the streaming response.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "The Insights engine ranks every AI recommendation by ROI, carbon impact, and implementation ease — powered by our trained recommendation model. And whenever you need context, the EcoVista AI assistant is one click away — it knows the entire platform, every module, and your campus's sustainability data."

---

## [1:42 – 1:55] CARBON + COMMUNITY

> **On screen:** Quick cut to **Carbon** page — show the scope breakdown pie chart and the net-zero countdown. Then quick cut to **Leaderboard** — show the podium and department rankings. Then quick cut to **Challenges** — show an active eco-challenge with participant count.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Carbon tracking covers all three scopes with a countdown to net-zero. And sustainability isn't just a top-down initiative — the Leaderboard and Challenges modules turn it into a campus-wide movement, letting departments and buildings compete on energy reduction."

---

## [1:55 – 2:10] CLOSING — TECH STACK + CALL TO ACTION

> **On screen:** Split screen or quick montage: show the Finance module briefly, then the KPIs radar chart, then back to the Dashboard. End on the EcoVista logo / landing page hero.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "EcoVista is built on React, TypeScript, and Supabase — fully open-source, deployable on Vercel in minutes. The ML training pipeline is included in the repository, so the models can be retrained as real campus data accumulates. This is EcoVista — the intelligence layer that Indian campuses need to reach net zero."

---

## 🎬 Production Notes

| Segment | Duration | Key UI focus |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Hook / Landing | 10s | Hero scroll |
| Login + Role | 12s | Login → dashboard role badge |
| Dashboard | 23s | KPI cards, trend arrows, building ranking |
| Energy Forecast | 27s | 72h chart with CI bands, What-If sliders, building radar |
| Mission Control | 16s | Toggle levers + intensity slider → trajectory chart |
| AI Insights + Chat | 14s | Recommendation cards + streaming AI chat |
| Carbon + Community | 13s | Scope pie, countdown, leaderboard podium |
| Closing | 15s | Finance, KPIs, logo |

**Total: ~2 min 10 sec**

---

> **Recording tips:**
> - Disable notifications before recording
> - Use browser zoom 90% so more of the UI fits
> - Pre-login before starting the recording so you don't waste time on password typing
> - For the AI chat segment, type the question before recording and paste it in during the take
